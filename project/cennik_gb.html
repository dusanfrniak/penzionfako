Accommodation Price List

The guesthouse and apartments are open all year round with a total capacity of 46 beds. The accommodation facility is divided into 2 separate parts:

1. Penzión FAKO

The guesthouse offers 24 fixed beds and 9 extra beds:
• 5 x double rooms (studio) with kitchenette and possibility of extra bed (2+1)
• 5 x double rooms (studio) with kitchenette
• 2 x fully equipped apartment (2+2)

Period 09.01. - 26.12. 27.12. - 10.1.
Studio adult (1 person) 28 € 35 €
Studio adult (2 persons) 45 € 60 €
Extra bed 10 € 15 €
Apartment / max 4 persons 70 € 90 €

2. FAKO Apartments

The apartments are located in a separate building, which is very close to the guesthouse.

There are 4 apartments available:
• 2 x apartment for 4 persons
• 1 x apartment for 3 persons
• 1 x apartment for 2 persons

Prices depend on the date and the number of persons in the apartment:
• 2 persons - 45 € / night
• 3 persons - 60 € / night
• 4 persons - 70 € / night

Prices are in EUR per 1 night including 10% VAT

• Children up to 6 years without claim to bed or extra bed stay free of charge.
• The accommodation price does not include the local tax of 1.50€ per person/night, applies to every person from 10 years old.
• Stays during the New Year's Eve period are minimum 7 nights.
• Discount possible for long-term stays.
• Discount possible for large groups.
