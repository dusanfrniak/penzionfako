Penzión FAKO

Penzión FAKO, with its stylish interior, quality services, and friendly staff, is suitable for families with children, young people, business travelers, groups, or individuals. Guests have rooms with full amenities, private bathroom and toilet, kitchenette, internet connection, covered garden pool, and free parking. Located in a quiet area of Liptovský Mikuláš, with easy access to the main route. Nearby are opportunities for sports and relaxation: Opalisko ski resort in Závažná Poruba, Monaco Grand Prix go-kart track, thermal pools in Liptovský Ján, Slovakia's largest aquapark Tatralandia, the most famous and largest ski resort Jasná, Demänová caves, cycling and hiking trails in Žiarska dolina. If you decide to stay in our facilities, you are warmly welcome. Accept our invitation and choose from our offer.

Penzión Fako - studios with balcony

Modern and functional double bed rooms, where you will find:
• LCD TV.
• satellite reception.
• free WiFi internet connection.
• private bathroom and toilet.
• large sunny balcony with seating.
• small kitchenette with equipment.

Penzión Fako - Apartments with balcony

The apartment consists of 2 rooms, a bedroom with two fixed beds and a living area with the possibility of 2-3 extra beds.

It is equipped with:
• LCD TV.
• satellite reception.
• free WiFi internet connection.
• fully equipped kitchen with dining table.
• private bathroom and toilet.
• large sunny balcony with seating.

Fako Apartments - without balcony
